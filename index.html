About

This historic public house situated a stone’s throw away from the famous Fosse way is to be found just off the Grantham canal and the 14th century St Luke’s parish church. The Inn welcomes all to experience our picturesque garden, quaint snug and rustic restaurant. With its popular events throughout the week and Sunday lunch menu the Plough Inn is a perfect venue for all special celebrations or just a speedy visit.
